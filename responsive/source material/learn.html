Controls

There are 5 buttons for the user to press at the bottom of the screen:Tap,Sing,Plug,Man and Pause.
Tap turns the tap on and off.
Sing makes the man sing a tune when he is in the bath.
Plug open and closes the plug hole.
Man places and takes away a man in the bath when it is pressed.
Click Pause to freeze the action and discuss the graph.
Try different combinations of the buttons and see what happens!

Aim

The aim of the game is to learn how to interpret a graph and
draw conclusions from them. Also how water levels change when different factors are altered.
